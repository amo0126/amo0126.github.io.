Lessons and Rates

Beginner

  • For nervous swimmers or swimmers new to the water
  • Works on gradual adaptation to the water
  • Focuses on:
    • Movement in the water
    • Blowing bubbles
    • Total body submersion
    • Floats (front and back)
    • Assisted kicking and paddling in the water

Intermediate

  • For swimmers that are comfortable in the water
  • Works on advancement to unassisted swimming
  • Focuses on:
    • Unassisted kicking and gliding while in float position
    • Advancement to freestyle/backstroke
    • Proper breathing and breath control

Advanced

  • For swimmers that can swim for 5-25 meters unassisted
  • Works on stroke refinement and advancement of swimming distance
  • Focuses on:
    • Freestyle stroke refinement
    • Backstroke stroke refinement
    • Introduction to breaststroke
    • Advancement to swimming >25 yards unassisted in 3/4 strokes
